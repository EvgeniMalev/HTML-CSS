Nismus

Since time immemorial, the planet has been home to tens of thousands of civilizations. Until 15,000,000 years ago, the Kolnite and Salite civilizations shared their lives on this planet. These were two humanoid species that were radically different from each other. The Colnites were the highest civilization created from 14 other civilizations, and that of the Salites was created from a total of 23 civilizations that unified over the last 12,000,000,000 years. The two civilizations conquered neighboring solar systems and yellow gas giants parallel to each other without knowing each other for 1,000,000,000 years. The ignorance of the civilizations about each other was due to the fact that both species existed from separate dimensions.
