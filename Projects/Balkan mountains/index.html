BALKAN MOUNTAINS

The Balkan mountain range is located in the eastern part of the Balkans in Southeastern Europe. The range is conventionally taken to begin at the peak of Vrashka Chuka on the border between Bulgaria and Serbia.

Buzludzha Monument

The Buzludzha Monument is a historical landmark located on Buzludzha Peak in the Balkan Mountains.

Central Balkan National Park

This national park is located in the heart of the Balkan Mountains and is known for its rich biodiversity and stunning landscapes.

Troyan Monastery

The Troyan Monastery is a beautiful and historic monastery located in the northern Balkan Mountains.
