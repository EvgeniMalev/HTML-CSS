Sepfen

The Sepfen is an ancient war hero issuing from Mount Zotris. They are characterized by blue skin and red eyes. They have spikes on their backs and possess incredible strength. They draw the most energy from minerals found in igneous rocks.

Zarek

The Zarek is a humanoid race inhabiting the Tasama Mountains and the lakes around the Nir Valley. It is characteristic of these humanoids that they can levitate and create magic, after years of practicing alchemy.

Malchorn

The Malchorn are a group of wizards inhabiting wastelands. Their gift is that they can move objects by making micro-frequency sounds. They can imitate the sounds of over 500 living creatures and thus deceive the enemy.
